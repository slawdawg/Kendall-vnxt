## What KNX Is

KNX is a local-first governance core for KendallAI vNext work. It helps keep assistant-module planning, evidence handling, boundary decisions, safety review, and optional-pack decisions explicit before implementation, distribution, or operational use.

## Why It Matters

KNX is designed to reduce accidental risk in assistant workflows by making these decisions visible and durable:

- what data and source material can be used,
- where generated evidence can be stored,
- what actions require approval,
- which optional packs are separate from core governance,
- what validation evidence exists,
- what remains blocked or provisional.

## Possible Next Steps Before Any Company Discussion

Before any company-facing discussion or sharing:

1. Confirm employment/IP and confidentiality constraints.
2. Decide the exact audience and sharing mechanism.
3. Create a legal-reviewed discussion or evaluation notice.
4. Run safety review against the exact final material.
5. Record explicit approval for the exact sharing event.
